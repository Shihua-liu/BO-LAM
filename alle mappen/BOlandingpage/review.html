Food art, in many different ways

The LAM museum only takes reservations, in small numbers, so book your trip in advance. The central theme is food, and through five consecutive rooms you get to see many different sides to it (with an occasional piece not linked to food). Some of the pieces of art will stay with you for a long time, because of their extreme emotionality or their unconventional perspective. The enthusiasm of the staff is contagious. We biked to the museum from Noordwijk, which definitely added to the experience!
